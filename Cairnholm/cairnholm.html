Cairnholm

Cairnholm is a small town and island located near Wales in the United Kingdom. During the Holocaust, Jacob's Jewish grandfather was sent as a child to Miss Peregrine's orphanage.

After Jacob gets guided by Dr. Golan in Englewood to learn more about his grandfather and find Miss Peregrine and the children. His father and him stay in the only rooms in Cairnholm called the Priest Hole. Once he gets to the island however, he learns that there was a bombing that destroyed the house and killed the children. After learning this, he gets directions from Martin's uncle Oggie.

After getting directions, Jacob goes with two people named Worm and Dylan (also known as Dirty D). They lead him to a sheep stable, then refuse to go further. Jacob goes to an old, scarred, and broken down house instead of the "magic house" that his grandfather always described.

Near the house, there was a bog. In the bog, there was a cairn where he wandered into a very different place.
