The Priest Hole

The priest hole is a hotel in Cairnholm where Jacob and his father stayed. Jacob snuck out occasionally to the cairn to see his friends in the loop.


Jacob's father usually stays up at the priest hole working on his book and drinking the beer. The food there is mediocre at best. Once Jacob's father notices a "birdwatcher" who is a wight he gives up on his book.


At the hotel, the "birdwatcher" wight visits and asks for some food. He also watches Jacob outside the priest hole.
