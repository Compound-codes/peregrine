The Cairn

After Jacob explores the the ruined orphanage for the second time, the children spot him and he chases Emma to the Cairn. He goes through the cairn, only to find himself in back in time in the loop.


After Emma teaches Jacob how to use the loop, he is able to enter and exit and go back to the town of Cairnholm on the island.


Later on in the story, Jacob kills the hollowgast with a pair of shears in the bog surrounding the cairn.
