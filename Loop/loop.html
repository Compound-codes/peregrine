Inside the Loop

Loops are isolated places both physically and temporally. The main loop discussed in this book is Miss Peregrine's, set in Cairnholm on September 3rd, 1940. One could enter and exit it through the tunnel near the cairn.

Loops are meant to protect peculiar children from the outside world's dangers, like hollowgasts.

A recurring place in the book is Cairnholm's harbor, specifically in the loop.
