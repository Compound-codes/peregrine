Englewood

Jacob and his family live in the town Englewood. Englewood is "the small, somnolent beach town where [Jacob] live[s]." (pg. 25).

Inside of Englewood is Smart Aid, a drugstore chain that Jacob's uncle owns. Jacob will eventually inherit this company. In the start of the book, he desparately tries to get fired from Smart Aid, but fails because of the aformentioned facts.

Jacob's grandfather also lives here, in a place named 'Circle Village.'

After Grandpa Portman is killed, Jacob is soon sent to a therapist, named Doctor Golan.

Eventually, he will head off the Cairnholm to search for answers about his grandfather.
